Why AfriMeet

Built different.
Built for us.

Every feature designed around African internet, African devices and African organizations.

Low Bandwidth Mode
Automatically adjusts video quality based on your connection. Works on 2G, 3G and unstable networks across Africa.
Pay with M-Pesa
No dollar cards needed. Pay with M-Pesa, MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money or Flutterwave in your local currency.
Data Stays in Africa
Your meetings, recordings and data are stored on African servers. Your conversations never leave the continent.
Up to 500 Participants
Host large community calls, town halls, university lectures and NGO meetings with hundreds of participants.
No App Download
Join from any browser on any device. No installation. No account required to join a meeting someone shares with you.
End-to-End Encrypted
Every call is encrypted. Your conversations are private, secure and protected — no surveillance, no data selling.

How it works

Three steps. No friction. No dollar card. No downloads.

1
Create a room
Type a room name or let AfriMeet generate one. Your room is ready in seconds — no signup required to start.
2
Share the link
Copy your meeting link and share via WhatsApp, SMS or email. Anyone with the link can join instantly from their browser.
3
Meet without struggle
Clear video, clear audio — even on 3G. AfriMeet automatically adjusts to your connection so the meeting never drops.
